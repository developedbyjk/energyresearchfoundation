Energy Research Foundation works to bring out Talent from the youth and provide them platform to showcase their talent. Moreover we Encourages Students by giving them Books, Uniform, and Scholarship.

Energy Research Foundation Arrange Functions, Seminars to spread awareness regarding energy saving and electricity and work under Government Guidance to generate Energy from Sea, Wind, Canal, Bio etc

Energy Research Foundation works to Spread Awareness Regarding Various Schemes of Government for the betterment of society

  • Establish new Energy Research with the help of State government and central government
  • Generate Energy from Rivers and canal
  • Establish Solar Projects
  • Provide electricity to village, mountains, hilly and desert areas
  • Run Primary School, High School and Colleges
  • Work to Empower women through Tailoring and Mehdi Classes
  • helps lower class of society
  • Work as per GEDA
